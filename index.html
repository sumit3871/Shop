- About -


Thw Quality CLoths is an emerging clothing line start-up that is geared towards female/Male professionals between the ages of 26 – 64. The clothing line is designed to be everyday work essentials with a modern and trendy flair, appropriate for the office or afterwards for cocktails . The clothing is designed for the modern woman who has a busy professional career and engaging social life.

Thw Quality CLoths is the brainchild of Boston native and Paris-trained fashion designer, Marla Worthington. Now living in New York, Marla has taken her decades of apprenticeship and design to develop her own unique and trendy clothing line, while maintaining the style and sophistication required of a professional woman.

The Thw Quality CLoths clothing line is suitable for women and men of all ages and body types. No matter what status level or background, there is a professional look suitable and affordable for all. The Quality CLoths clothing line can be found at major retail department stores around the United States and India.
